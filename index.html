How To Guide
Questions

Think of a question to ask the 8 ball. It could be something that you are trying to decide on or could just be for fun. To get you started, some example questions could include:

  • Should I eat a burger for dinner?
  • Will I ever own a cat?
  • Should I learn how to speak Japanese?
  • Will I die from old age?
  • Will I pass my exam?
  • Should I accept the job offer?
  • Will I ever learn to love again?
  • Should I buy a croissant?
  • Will it rain tomorrow?
