Ask and shake the 8 Ball

Need to make a decision? Or possibly looking for a bit of retro fun? Look no further, the 8 ball oracle is here! Simply ask a Yes or No question, and click on the ball to see the result. Move your mouse around to see the ball animate, or click to see it rotate and display one of the classic results from the original 8 ball novelty toy.

How To Play

Guide

Shake the 8 ball (inspired by the Mattel toy 'Magic 8 Ball') is a question/answer based game.

  1. To play, think of a question that results in a 'Yes' or 'No' answer.
  2. Click or Tap anywhere on the 8 ball to shake.
  3. The 8 ball will shake and rotate to reveal the answer.
  4. If you received a non-committal answer, if you think of another question or if you simply don't like the answer, click the anywhere on 8 ball to shake and play again.
  5. Remember, this is a game, you are in control of your own decisions.

Questions

Think of a question to ask the 8 ball. It could be something that you are trying to decide on or could just be for fun. To get you started, some example questions could include:

  • Should I eat a burger for dinner?
  • Will I ever own a cat?
  • Should I learn how to speak Japanese?
  • Will I die from old age?
  • Will I pass my exam?
  • Should I accept the job offer?
  • Will I ever learn to love again?
  • Should I buy a croissant?
  • Will it rain tomorrow?
Tip! Always ask a 'Yes' or 'No' question - this ensures that the answers are in the right context. Remember, this is a game, you are in control of your own decisions.
