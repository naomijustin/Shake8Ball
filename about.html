Retro fun for the web

Inspired by the Mattel's original Magic 8 Ball, Shake 8 Ball brings the nostalgic toy to life with a modern spin.

Shake 8 Ball was built as a retro toy to relive the days of bringing out the 8 ball in your group of friends and shaking to ask questions about what you'd be when you grow up, if your crush liked you back, or if your parents would let you go to that sleepover on Saturday.

Like the original Magic 8 Ball, Shake 8 Ball has a set of prewritten answers that are displayed at random each time you shake the 8 ball. The set includes 10 positive answers, 5 neutral answers and 5 negative answers.

Shake 8 ball does not have fortune-telling abilities and its purpose is to only be used as a toy or game.

Play on the go or share with your friends the classic toy that gripped the world! Shake the 8 ball now!
